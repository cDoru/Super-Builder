Super Builder

This tool can only be used in Chrome!
Left-clicking to divide the unit Vertically. Right-clicking to devide the unit Horizontally.
Ctrl + Right-clicking to delete the unit which your mouse hovers on.
Ctrl + Left-clicking to add a specific table with defined Rows: and Cols: .		 Left-clicking to add an sequence to an img. Right-clicking to delete selected sequence.
Click Ctrl + Left-click on the unit hovered to call a text editor, then you can add text with it.
Click on Select images to multi-select the images. Ctrl + Right-clicking to delete the hovered img.		 Pleast paste data from CWS into the relative column on your right. Click convert
Fill the blank on the upper-left of the img according to the table appeared in the middle.
If you do not want to add <a> to the img, just type "n" in the blank.		 Paste/Type Alt into the box. Special characters & Breaks will be replaced!
Press Ctrl + Q to insert words from the alias of this images (if clickable)
Press shift to make selected words upper/lower-case, Alt+F1/F2/F3 to insert ®/™/©.
